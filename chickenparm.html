Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

This is a classic chicken parmesan recipe that is a favorite of many.It is a great dish to enjoy with family and friends or to have later in the week as leftovers. It is a very simple recipe that is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with everyone.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of marinara
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup basil
  • 1/4 cup parsley

Steps to making Chicken Parm

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine breadcrumbs and parmesan in a bowl
  3. Dip chicken in breadcrumb mixture
  4. Heat olive oil in skillet
  5. Cook chicken in skillet until browned
  6. Place chicken in baking dish
  7. Top with mozzarella and marinara
  8. Bake for 20 minutes
  9. Top with basil and parsley
  10. Serve and ENJOY!

Notes

This recipe is great with a side of pasta, garlic bread, or a fresh green salad. Feel free to add any additional seasonings or ingredients to make it your own!
