Baked Ziti Recipe

Description

This is a classic baked ziti recipe that is a favorite of many. It is a great dish to make for a large group of people or to have as leftovers for the week. It is a simple recipe that is easy to make and is sure to be a hit with everyone. It is my favorite dish personally as I am Italian.

Ingredients

  • Dried ziti pasta
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 jars of spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces of provolone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups of sour cream
  • 6 ounces of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic salt
  • 2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup of chopped parsley
  • 1 egg beaten

Steps

To make baked ziti, you first need to cook the ziti pasta until al dente, then mix it with a combination of ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and some of your tomato sauce; layer the pasta mixture in a baking dish with the remaining sauce, top with additional mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
